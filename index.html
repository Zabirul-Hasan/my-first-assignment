Bangabandhu Satellite-1. The Bangabandhu Satellite-1 is Bangladesh's first satellite for communication and broadcasting. It is named after the nation's founder, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman. The satellite was made by Thales Alenia Space and launched on May 12, 2018, from Kennedy Space Center in the USA.
